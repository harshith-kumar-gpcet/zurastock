Master the art of AI-driven trading with this comprehensive guide.

1. Understanding AI Signals

BUY Strong Bullish
AI detects high momentum and positive sentiment. This usually indicates a good entry point if the confidence score is above 80%.
SELL Bearish Alert
AI detects potential price drops or overbought conditions. Consider taking profits or setting tight stop-losses.
HOLD Neutral Consolidation
The market is indecisive. It's best to wait for a clearer signal. Great for observing a stock before jumping in.

2. Technical Indicators

RSI (14)
Below 30: Oversold (Buying candidate).
Above 70: Overbought (Selling candidate).
SMA (20)
The 20-day average price. If the current price crosses ABOVE this, it's a "Golden Cross" (Bullish).
Confidence Score
Our AI's "conviction". High scores (>85%) mean technicals, sentiment, and fundamentals are all in sync.

The "When to Move" Cheat Sheet

When to BUY?
Signal is BUY + Confidence > 80% + RSI is below 40. This is the "Optimal Zone".
When to SELL?
Signal is SELL + RSI is above 70 + Negative news sentiment. This protects your gains.
When to WAIT?
Signal is HOLD or Confidence is low (< 50%). Market noise is too high; stay safe!
